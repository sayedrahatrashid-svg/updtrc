Common questions

Questions buyers usually ask
before they inquire.

Can you handle bulk corporate orders?

+

Yes — bulk corporate orders are exactly what REYCO Group is built for. Whether it's fifty curated kits for a leadership offsite, five hundred onboarding sets for a global rollout, or recurring quarterly orders for ongoing client appreciation, we plan production, sourcing, and timelines specifically around business-scale volumes. Quantities can scale from 50 units up to several thousand, with pricing structured to reward larger and repeat commitments. Lead times, packaging consistency, and presentation quality are managed end-to-end, so every recipient — whether they are the first one or the five hundredth — receives the same polished experience.

Can the products be branded with our company identity?

+

Yes — branding is one of the core areas where REYCO Group adds real value. We apply your company identity across both the products themselves (engraving, debossing, screen printing, embroidery, and full-color printing, choosing the right method for each material) and the presentation layer (custom boxes, sleeves, ribbons, internal cards, dividers, and tissue paper). Brand application is treated as a design exercise rather than a stamp-it-on afterthought, so the final output reflects how your company would actually want to be seen. We work from your existing brand guidelines or, where helpful, suggest treatments that elevate perceived quality without diluting your identity. Samples and proofs are signed off before any production run begins.

Do you help choose the right combination of items?

+

Yes — and this is something most clients lean on us for. If you don't already have a fixed product list, we shape the gift around four things: who is receiving it, what occasion or moment it is for, what budget per recipient feels right, and what impression you want the gift to leave behind. From there we suggest a combination that balances usefulness, perceived value, and visual cohesion — typically three to six items per set, chosen so they feel curated rather than collected. We also flag what doesn't work: items that look generic, items that don't travel well, or items that quietly cheapen the rest of the box. The goal is a set that feels intentional from the moment it is opened.

What kinds of gifting projects are best suited to REYCO?

+

REYCO is best suited to projects where presentation matters as much as the product itself — client appreciation gifts during contract milestones or year-end; onboarding kits for new hires that signal company culture from day one; visitor and VIP welcome boxes for office or event guests; conference and trade-show packs that need to make an impression at scale; partner and channel gifts that reinforce ongoing relationships; executive and milestone gifting for leadership-level recognition; and seasonal campaign packs tied to specific marketing moments. We are less suited to one-off, low-budget novelty merchandise — but anything where your brand is being represented in someone's hands, we can elevate considerably.

How long do orders typically take?

+

Lead times depend on quantity, customization complexity, and shipping route, but a typical bulk order moves from confirmed brief to delivered product in four to eight weeks. Smaller orders with off-the-shelf items can move faster — sometimes within two to three weeks. Heavily customized sets with bespoke packaging and printed branding sit at the longer end. We always confirm a realistic timeline upfront, build in a buffer for sampling and approval rounds, and flag risks early rather than at the last minute. For time-sensitive launches and events, we recommend reaching out at least eight weeks in advance to give the production schedule room to breathe.

Do you ship internationally? What's the minimum order?

+

Yes — REYCO Group ships to companies across Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and North America, with operations coordinated from our offices in Dhaka, Kuala Lumpur, and London. Minimum order quantities depend on the level of customization: standard branded items can start from around fifty units, while fully bespoke gift sets with custom packaging usually start at one hundred units. Shipping is handled directly to your office, your event venue, or distributed individually to recipients — whichever fits your operation. International logistics, customs documentation, and consolidated delivery are all part of what we manage on your behalf so your team isn't chasing freight forwarders the week of an event.
